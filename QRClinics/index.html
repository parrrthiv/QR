📋 Government Proposal Document
QRClinics PHC Upgrade Proposal

Submitted to: NHM Kerala · District Health Office, Ernakulam · Kerala Startup Mission

NHM കേരള · ജില്ലാ ആരോഗ്യ ഓഫീസ്, എറണാകുളം

🎯 The Problem

Kerala's 1,276 PHCs are the first point of care for 3.4 crore citizens. Yet 78% lack specialist access on-site. Patients travel 2–4 hours to tertiary hospitals for conditions manageable at PHC level with remote specialist support.

78% PHC-കളിൽ സ്പെഷ്യലിസ്റ്റ് ഇല്ല. രോഗികൾ ടെർഷ്യറി ആശുപത്രിയിൽ 2–4 മണിക്കൂർ യാത്ര ചെയ്യുന്നു.

💡 The Solution — QRClinics PHC Model

Each PHC gets a dedicated WhatsApp number, AI triage system, video consult with specialist board, and Jan Aushadhi prescription delivery. Zero disruption to existing PHC staff workflow.

ഓരോ PHC-ക്കും WhatsApp, AI, വീഡിയോ കൺസൾട്ട്, Jan Aushadhi ഡെലിവറി.

🗓️ Implementation Roadmap
1
Month 1–2: Pilot (2 PHCs)

Kalamassery + Aluva PHC. WhatsApp setup, specialist onboarding, staff training, ABHA integration.

2
Month 3–6: District Rollout (12 PHCs)

All Ernakulam pilot PHCs. MDT Board activation, Jan Aushadhi integration, NHM reporting live.

3
Month 6–18: Kerala Statewide

Scale to all 1,276 Kerala PHCs. Integration with CM Health Insurance Scheme, Karunya Health.

4
Year 2+: National Replication

NHM India model. Replicate across 25,743 PHCs nationally. NHSRC policy brief submission.
